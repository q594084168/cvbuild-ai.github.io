How to Write a Great Startup Resume Builder
  1. Lead with versatility: highlight the multiple roles you've played
  2. Quantify growth: users, revenue, team size from 0 to scale
  3. Show scrappiness: built from scratch, zero-to-one achievements
  4. Include cross-functional impact across product, sales, ops, and support
  5. Mention fundraising milestones, investor relationships, or incubator participation

Tips for Better Results
Startup experience is gold if framed correctly. Don't apologize for wearing many hats — that's the strength. Show impact across functions: 'Built support process that scaled from 10 to 500 tickets/week without adding headcount.' Investors and acquirers love operators who can build systems from chaos.
